# Potentiometers

[potentiometer explained](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=sWbSeJmUFfw)

- variable resistor or voltage divider
- 2 terminals --> variable resistor
- 3 terminals --> voltage divider
- components: wiper, terminal, resistive material

Variable resistor
- 2 terminal usage
- resistance proportional to amount of resistive material included in circuit
- 2 resistors in series
- `Rt = R2 + R1`
